# Lumina-AI

## The Idea

**Lumina-AI** is an AI-powered Career Operating System designed to bridge the opportunity gap for women by providing personalized career guidance, learning resources, mentorship, scholarships, internships, job discovery, resume building, and financial empowerment in one unified platform.

Many women struggle with scattered resources, limited mentorship, and difficulty navigating career opportunities. Lumina-AI solves this by acting as an intelligent career companion that understands each user's goals, skills, education, and aspirations to deliver personalized recommendations, structured career roadmaps, and continuous support throughout their professional journey.

# Features

* **AI Career Coach** – Personalized career guidance, goal planning, interview preparation, and skill recommendations.
* **Smart Resume Builder** – Generates ATS-friendly resumes with AI-powered suggestions.
* **Scholarship & Opportunity Finder** – Recommends scholarships, internships, fellowships, and competitions based on user profiles.
* **Job & Internship Matching** – Matches users with relevant opportunities using AI.
* **Personalized Learning Hub** – Suggests courses, certifications, and learning resources to close skill gaps.
* **Mentor Connect** – Enables users to connect with mentors and industry professionals.
* **Financial Wellness Assistant** – Offers guidance on budgeting, education funding, and financial planning.
* **Progress Dashboard** – Tracks goals, learning progress, applications, and achievements.
* **Intelligent Onboarding** – Creates a personalized career profile through AI-driven onboarding.
* **Responsive Design** – Optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

# Documentation

## How It Works

Users begin by signing in with Google and completing an AI-powered onboarding process. During onboarding, Lumina-AI collects information such as education, skills, interests, experience, preferred career paths, and professional goals.

This information is securely stored in Firebase and used to build a personalized career profile.

Based on this profile, the AI generates customized recommendations, including:

* Career roadmaps
* Job and internship opportunities
* Scholarships
* Learning resources
* Resume improvements
* Interview preparation
* Skill development plans

As users update their profiles or achieve milestones, the recommendation engine continuously adapts to provide relevant and up-to-date guidance.

---

## AI Workflow

Lumina-AI integrates AI across multiple modules instead of limiting it to a single chatbot.

The AI system:

* Understands user profiles and career aspirations.
* Generates personalized career roadmaps.
* Matches users with relevant jobs, internships, and scholarships.
* Identifies skill gaps and recommends learning resources.
* Optimizes resumes for ATS compatibility.
* Assists with interview preparation through role-specific questions and feedback.

This integrated approach creates a personalized career ecosystem that supports users from exploration and learning to employment and long-term career growth.
